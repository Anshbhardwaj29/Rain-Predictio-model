Rain Prediction Form

Instructions

  • Humidity3pm: Enter the percentage humidity of the wind at 3 PM.
  • RainToday: Enter 1 if it rained today, or 0 if it did not.
  • Cloud3pm: Enter the cloud cover at 3 PM in eighths (0 to 8). 0 means no cloud, 8 means full cloud cover.
  • Humidity9am: Enter the percentage humidity of the wind at 9 AM.
  • Cloud9am: Enter the cloud cover at 9 AM in eighths (0 to 8).
  • Rainfall: Specify the amount of rainfall in millimeters.
  • WindGustSpeed: Enter the speed of the strongest gust during the day in kilometers per hour.
  • Ensure all fields are completed before submitting the form.
